CARP Project

Contrast Alignment

I created a Contrast poster by taking inspiration from several music festival posters color schemes such as rolling loud I managed to create a contrast by presenting contrasting colours that have very little corralation. I also presented my text in contrasting ways such as size and style.

I created an Alignment poster by making a camera that is positioned virtically aligned to the right of the text while also demonstrating the text in an aligned fashion having it the same height as the camera by having the text in diffrent sizes yet keeping the strict line by line Formation.

Repetition Proximity

I created a Repetition poster by creating a long line of film tape and adding it across the page and also repeating this under or ontop of some of the text in this piece. I also took inspiration on the color scheme from the graphic novel the Watchmen especially on the Film festival logo.

I created a Proximity poster by taking insperation from the karate kid movie poster which inspired my color scheme and to increase the logo size to create a large sense of proximity I also added cinema chairs infront of the logo to demostrate perseption to increase the effect of proximity.
